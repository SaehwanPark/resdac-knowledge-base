The Original Medicare Fee-for-Service (FFS) Durable Medical Equipment (DME) file contains fee-for-service claims submitted by Durable Medical Equipment suppliers to the DME Medicare Administrative Contractor (MAC).
What does this file include? (variable highlights)
- Diagnosis, (ICD diagnosis code)
- Services provided (CMS Common Procedure Coding System (HCPCS) codes)
- Dates of service
- Charge
- Reimbursement amounts
- Supplier provider number
- Supplier NPI
